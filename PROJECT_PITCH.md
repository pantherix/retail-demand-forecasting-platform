# Project Pitch

## Title

Retail Demand Forecasting and Inventory Risk Platform using Deep Time-Series Models

## Problem

Retail planners need to avoid two expensive outcomes: stock-outs that lose revenue and overstock that locks capital in unsold inventory.

## Solution

This platform forecasts SKU-level demand, benchmarks multiple forecasting models, and converts predictions into inventory risk recommendations.

## What Makes It Strong

- industry-specific use case instead of a generic forecasting notebook
- model leaderboard by SKU
- stock-out and overstock risk scoring
- planner-friendly recommendations
- API and dashboard structure
- integration path for Time-Series-Library deep models

## Interview Explanation

I transformed a research-focused time-series library into a retail decision platform. The system predicts demand, compares forecasting models, and translates forecast outputs into practical inventory actions such as reorder quantity, stock-out risk, and overstock warnings.

## Best Resume Bullet

Built a retail demand forecasting platform that benchmarks time-series models, forecasts SKU-level demand, and identifies stock-out/overstock risk with actionable inventory recommendations.
